About the Japan Self-Defense Force

The Japan Self-Defense Forces (JSDF) were established in 1954 and consist of the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force (JGSDF), the Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF), and the Japan Air Self-Defense Force (JASDF). The JSDF is controlled by the Ministry of Defense, with the Prime Minister as its commander-in-chief.

Mission Statement

The JSDF's primary mission is to defend Japan from external threats and contribute to international peacekeeping operations.

Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF)

The Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force is one of the world's largest navies, with a focus on anti-submarine warfare and maritime security.

Destroyers and Carriers

Maya-class Destroyer

Advanced Aegis-equipped destroyer with enhanced missile defense capabilities.

Kongō-class Destroyer

First generation of Aegis-equipped destroyers in the JMSDF.

Atago-class Destroyer

Modified version of the Kongō class equipped with the Aegis Combat System.

Izumo-class Carrier

Largest vessels in the JMSDF fleet, capable of carrying helicopters and F-35B fighters.

Hyūga-class Carrier

Helicopter carrier with advanced anti-submarine warfare capabilities.

Murasame-class Destroyer

This is the first class of the second-generation, general-purpose destroyers of the JMSDF.

Asagiri-class Destroyer

Second class of first-generation general-purpose destroyers of the JMSDF.

Akizuki-class Destroyer

Multi-purpose destroyer equipped with the ATECS combat system.

Mogami-class Frigate

Modern multi-mission frigate with stealth features.

Submarines

Taigei-class Submarine

Latest diesel-electric attack submarine with lithium-ion batteries.

Sōryū-class Submarine

Advanced diesel-electric attack submarine with AIP system.

Oyashio-class Submarine

Diesel-electric attack submarine with advanced sonar systems.

Air Self-Defense Force (JASDF)

The Japan Air Self-Defense Force maintains a modern fleet of fighter jets, transport aircraft, and early warning systems to protect Japan's airspace.

Fighter Aircraft

F-35 Lightning II

Fifth-generation stealth multirole fighter. Japan operates both the F-35A and F-35B variants.

F-15J Eagle

Air superiority fighter, backbone of Japan's air defense for decades.

F-2

Multirole fighter developed with Mitsubishi Heavy Industries and Lockheed Martin, based on the F-16 with larger wing surface.

Support Aircraft

Boeing E-767

Airborne Warning and Control System based on Boeing 767 airframe.

E-2D Advanced Hawkeye

Airborne early warning aircraft with powerful radar systems.

C-2 Transport

Indigenously developed transport aircraft by Kawasaki Heavy Industries.

Ground Self-Defense Force (JGSDF)

The Japan Ground Self-Defense Force is responsible for the defense of the Japanese islands from ground invasion and works in coordination with the other branches of the JSDF.

Tanks and Armored Vehicles

Type 10 MBT

Latest generation main battle tank with advanced C4I systems and modular armor.

Type 90 MBT

Main battle tank featuring a 120mm smoothbore gun and composite armor.

Type 16 MCV

Wheeled tank destroyer with high mobility for rapid deployment.

Type 89 IFV

Infantry Fighting Vehicle with 35mm autocannon.

Type 96 APC

8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier with improved protection.

Type 19 Wheeled SPH

Modern wheeled 155mm self-propelled howitzer for rapid deployment.

Mitsubishi Type 73 light truck

Series of military light trucks that are used as mini SUVs in the JSDF.

Type 96 multi-purpose missile system

Anti-tank/landing craft missile used by the JGSDF. It is the first Japanese missile system to use a complete digitally controlled interface..

Type 87 self-propelled anti-aircraft gun

Japanese air defense weapon built around the Oerlikon 35 mm twin cannon system as used on the Gepard self-propelled anti-aircraft gun. The system uses a modified Type 74 tank chassis.

Helicopters

AH-64DJP Apache

Advanced attack helicopter with Japanese modifications.

UH-60JA Black Hawk

Utility helicopter for troop transport and logistics.

OH-1 Ninja

Indigenously developed observation helicopter.

Military Technology

The JSDF utilizes advanced military technology, much of which is developed domestically by Japanese defense contractors.

Defense Systems

Type 12 SSM

Advanced anti-ship missile with terrain-following capabilities and extended range.

SM-3 Block IIA

Advanced ballistic missile interceptor developed jointly with the US.

Type 03 SAM

Medium-range surface-to-air missile system for air defense.

XASM-3 Missile

Supersonic anti-ship missile with advanced targeting capabilities.

M270

Armored self-propelled multiple launch rocket system.

JADGE System

Integrated air defense network connecting radar sites across Japan.
